Muning Wen is a Research Assistant Professor at the School of Artificial Intelligence, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. He completed his Ph.D. in June 2025 at the Department of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. His research interests are centered on reinforcement learning, large model agents, and multi-agent systems. Throughout his academic career, Muning has been dedicated to developing advanced reinforcement learning algorithms to enhance the sequential decision-making capabilities of autonomous agents and multi-agent systems in dynamic environments. He has also been actively involved in applying these algorithms to domains such as programming, mathematics, and mobile agents. Muning's papers have been published in top-tier academic conferences, and he serves as a regular reviewer for numerous conferences and journals, such as NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, AAAI, AAMAS, and TPAMI.
